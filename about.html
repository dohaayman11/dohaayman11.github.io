01 — At a Glance

A multidisciplinary contractor built for the Kingdom's ambitions.

ROOTBUILD is a multidisciplinary contracting company established to deliver integrated construction and finishing solutions across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We specialize in architectural and interior design, fire-fighting and safety systems, infrastructure works, insulation systems, and epoxy flooring — supported by a complete fit-out, finishing, furnishing and maintenance capability.

Drawing on the combined experience of our founders and engineering teams gained through major projects in Egypt, the wider region and abroad, ROOTBUILD brings proven know-how to a market driven by Saudi Vision 2030 and one of the most ambitious construction pipelines in the world.

As our name suggests, we believe great structures begin with strong roots: disciplined planning, sound engineering and an uncompromising commitment to our clients. Every member of our team adds to ROOTBUILD.
