02 — Our Project Delivery Approach

A structured, stage-gated process.

We follow a structured, stage-gated delivery process that keeps every project aligned on quality, time and cost from first brief to final handover.

1

Understand

We study the client's vision, requirements, site conditions and budget to define a clear, shared scope.

2

Design & Plan

We develop functional designs with cost studies, time schedules and structural/MEP outlines.

3

Procure

We secure materials, equipment and qualified subcontractors through our trusted supply chain.

4

Execute

We deliver works to specification under strict quality control and a zero-harm safety regime.

5

Commission & Handover

We test, commission, snag and hand over a fully integrated, ready-to-use facility.

6

Maintain

We support the asset through warranty, planned maintenance and renovation services.

03 — Quality, Health, Safety & Environment (QHSE)

Not a department. How we work.

Quality and safety are not departments at ROOTBUILD — they are how we work. We are committed to delivering every project to the highest standards while protecting our people, our clients and the environment.

Quality

Documented quality plans, ITPs and method statements for each package. Compliance with the Saudi Building Code (SBC).

Health & Safety

A zero-harm culture with site-specific HSE plans, trained personnel, PPE enforcement and emergency-response procedures.

Environment

Responsible waste management, resource efficiency, and dust/noise control aligned with the Kingdom's environmental goals.
