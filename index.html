01 — About Us

Disciplined planning. Sound engineering.
Uncompromising commitment.

ROOTBUILD is a multidisciplinary contracting company delivering integrated construction and finishing solutions across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We specialize in architectural and interior design, fire-fighting and safety systems, infrastructure works, insulation systems, and epoxy flooring — supported by a complete fit-out, finishing, furnishing and maintenance capability.

As our name suggests, we believe great structures begin with strong roots: disciplined planning, sound engineering and an uncompromising commitment to our clients. Every member of our team adds to ROOTBUILD.

02 — Why ROOTBUILD

A focused contractor with major-project discipline

Our clients choose ROOTBUILD because we combine the agility of a focused contractor with the discipline of a major-project organization — one accountable partner from design through finishing, furnishing and maintenance.
